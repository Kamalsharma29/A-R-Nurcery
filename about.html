Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Saharanpur, Ar Rehman Nursery is a sanctuary for plant lovers. We are a family-run business with a deep-rooted passion for horticulture, specializing in a wide variety of unique and exotic plants, especially fruit trees that thrive in the local climate.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring nature closer to people and to cultivate a greener, healthier community. Today, we are proud to have served over 10,000 happy customers and share our passion with a growing audience on YouTube.
